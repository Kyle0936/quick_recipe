## Ingredients
- 2 cups flour
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 cup blueberries
- 1 lemon (zest)
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 cup milk

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 375°F / 190°C.
2. Mix dry ingredients, then fold in wet ingredients and blueberries.
3. Fill muffin tins and bake for 20-22 minutes.
4. Cool and serve.
