## Ingredients
- 12 lasagna sheets
- 500g minced beef
- 2 cups tomato sauce
- 2 cups ricotta
- 2 cups mozzarella

## Instructions
1. Brown the beef and combine with tomato sauce.
2. Layer sheets, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella.
3. Bake at 375°F / 190°C for 45 minutes.
4. Rest for 10 minutes and serve.
