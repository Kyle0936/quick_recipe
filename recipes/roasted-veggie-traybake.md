## Ingredients
- 1 cauliflower head
- 2 zucchinis
- 1 red onion
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Chop vegetables and toss with oil and spices.
2. Roast at 400°F / 205°C for 30-35 minutes.
3. Serve with quinoa or rice.
